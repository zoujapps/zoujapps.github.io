Privacy Policy · Profit Track Pro

Last updated: May 12, 2026

At ZoujApps (contact: zoujapps@gmail.com), your privacy is our top priority. This document explains how Profit Track Pro handles any data you enter or store on your device.

1. Data We Collect

The app does not collect any personal or financial data on our external servers. All data you enter (e.g., financial records, preferred currency, settings, backups) is stored locally only on your mobile device using a local SQLite database. You are the sole controller of this data.

2. How We Use Data

Your local data is used only to power core app features:

  • Display daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly profit/loss summaries.
  • Access archives up to 10 years back.
  • Apply your preferences (interface language, currency, light/dark theme).
  • Create local backups and optionally share them (via file sharing) to restore later or move to another device.

We do not analyze, sell, or share your data with any third party.

3. Internet Connection

The current version of the app requires an internet connection for license verification (future optional cloud features will ask for your explicit permission). No financial or personal data is ever transmitted over the internet.

4. Backup & Import

The app allows you to export all your data (records & settings) into a backup file created on your device. You may share this file as you wish. You can also import it later either by merging (combining new data with existing) or full replacement. This entire process happens locally on your device – we never see or store these files.

5. App Permissions

The app requests only basic permissions such as storage (to save/restore backups) and internet (for verification, not to collect data). It does not request access to contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children – and as explained, the app does not send any data to us. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with data, contact us immediately (though no data is transmitted to us).

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via an app update or an in-app notice. Your continued use after modifications means you accept the updated policy.

🔒 Privacy in a nutshell:
All your data stays on your device. We don’t track you, follow you, or sell your info. You control your backup files.

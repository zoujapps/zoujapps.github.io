Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

📌 Introduction

At Wake Enforcer, we take your privacy seriously. This app is designed to be secure and transparent — we do not collect any personal data. All information stays locally on your device only.

📷 Camera Usage Required

The app uses the camera only to take a photo of your sink or faucet as part of the wake-up challenge. This photo is not stored on your device or any server, and it is not shared with any third party.

✅ The photo is used solely to verify that you have physically gotten up, and is discarded immediately.

🔐 Permissions Required

  • Camera – to capture a photo of the sink/faucet for verification.
  • Notifications – to send alarm alerts.
  • Vibration – for alarm vibration.
  • Foreground Service – to keep the alarm running even in the background.
  • Exact Alarm Scheduling – to ensure the alarm rings at the exact time you set.
  • Full-Screen Intent – to display the alarm screen over the lock screen.

📊 Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymized data to improve ad relevance and quality. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

💾 Data Storage

All data generated by the app (alarm settings, preferences, etc.) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is sent to any external server or third party.

🔗 Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements.
  • Google ML Kit – for on-device image labeling (runs entirely on your device, no data is sent to Google).

Privacy Policy for Wake Enforcer

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Wake Enforcer ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Camera Permission

The app uses the camera only to take a photo of a sink or faucet as part of the wake-up challenge. This photo is not stored on your device or any server, and it is not shared with anyone. It is used exclusively for verification purposes within the app.

3. Permissions Required

  • Camera: To take a photo of the sink/faucet for verification.
  • Notifications: To send alarm notifications.
  • Vibration: For alarm vibration.
  • Foreground Service: To keep the alarm running and ringing.
  • Schedule Exact Alarm: To schedule alarms accurately.

4. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymized data to serve personalized ads. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage

All data (alarm settings, preferences) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is sent to any server or third party.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – for advertising.
  • Google ML Kit – for image labeling (runs locally on device, no data sent to Google).

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@zoujapps.com

ZoujApps ("we", "our", "us") built the Brick Smash mobile application (the "App") as a free game supported by advertisements. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decides to use our App.

📌 Information Collection and Use

Brick Smash does not collect, store, or share any personal information that can directly identify you (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

However, the App uses third-party services (Google AdMob) that may collect non-personally identifiable information to serve relevant advertisements.

📱 Permissions Used

  • INTERNET – Required to load ads and connect to the internet.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – To check network connectivity for ads.
  • VIBRATE – To provide haptic feedback on certain interactions (optional).

📊 Google AdMob & Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Advertising Policy.

Data collected by AdMob may include:

  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size).
  • Approximate location (based on IP address).
  • Advertising ID (used for targeting and frequency capping).
  • App usage and interaction data.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization on Android).

💾 Local Storage (SharedPreferences)

The App stores only local game progress on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Best score
  • Current level progress
  • First installation date (used solely for ad activation timing)

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

🌍 Compliance with Moroccan Law (Law 09-08)

As a developer based in Morocco, we comply with Law 09-08 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data. Since the App does not collect or store personal data, it is exempt from registration with the Commission Nationale de Contrôle de la Protection des Données à Caractère Personnel (CNDP).

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

At ZoujApps, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how "Bubble Pop Legends" handles your data when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you (such as name, email, phone number, address, or location).

  • Non-personal usage data: We may collect anonymous data such as device type, OS version, and basic game interactions (e.g., rounds played) to improve performance. This data is not linked to your identity.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to serve interstitial ads. AdMob may use non-personal data (e.g., advertising ID) to provide personalized ads. You can review Google's privacy policy here.
  • Local Storage: The app uses Shared Preferences to store your settings (sound, vibration, aim guide) and high score locally on your device. This data is never shared with third parties.

2. App Permissions

  • Internet: Required for loading ads and checking connectivity.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback (can be disabled in settings).
  • No dangerous permissions (e.g., location, camera, contacts) are requested.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party, except as necessary for ad serving (via Google AdMob) and to comply with legal requirements.

4. Data Security

All data we store remains on your device only. We follow standard practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. External Links

The app may contain links to external sites (e.g., app store, developer website). We do not control the privacy practices of these sites, so we advise you to read their policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via the app or our website. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

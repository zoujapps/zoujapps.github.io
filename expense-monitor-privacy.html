Privacy Policy for Expense Monitor

Last updated: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Expense Monitor – Daily Loss Tracker (“the App”), developed by ZoujApps. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. All data you enter into the App (such as daily loss amounts, expense records, currency preferences, and language settings) is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Internet & Network State: Required for displaying advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • Notifications: To send you daily reminders to log your losses (optional, can be disabled in device settings).

No other sensitive permissions are required.

4. Data Storage and Backup

Your loss records are stored in local JSON files within the App’s private storage. Android's Auto Backup feature may back up this data to your Google Drive (linked to your Google account), allowing restoration if you reinstall the App. This backup is encrypted and accessible only by you. You can disable backup in your device settings.

5. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and process non-personal data (such as device information, coarse location, and ad interaction metrics) to provide relevant ads. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share any personal data with advertisers.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here.

6. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external sites (such as the Google Play Store for rating or sharing). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its storage manually via your device settings. Uninstalling the App removes all associated local data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: zoujapps@gmail.com

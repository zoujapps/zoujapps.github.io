Driver Assistant

Smart Vehicle Maintenance Tracker

📲 Download on Google Play

📱 App Overview

Driver Assistant helps you never miss a service for your car, motorcycle, bus, or truck. Keep track of oil changes, filter replacements, timing belt, and more — all in one simple dashboard.

✅ Multi-vehicle support with custom icons
✅ Automatic odometer updates using daily average distance
✅ Smart notifications 500 km/mi before maintenance is due
✅ Works offline – all data stays on your device

⚙️ Key Features

📊 Maintenance Tracking Oil, filters, transmission oil, timing belt with custom intervals.
⏰ Smart Reminders Repeat every 48h until you record the change.
📈 Daily Average Auto-updates odometer even when app is closed.
🌐 Multi-language English, Arabic, French, Spanish, Portuguese.
🔒 Privacy First No data collection. Everything is stored locally.
📤 Share & Rate Built-in buttons to share app and rate on Play Store.

🔐 Privacy Policy

Driver Assistant does not collect any personal information. All data (vehicle names, odometer readings, maintenance records) is stored exclusively on your device. No data is sent to any server or third party.

The app requests only notification permission to send maintenance reminders. It does not access location, camera, contacts, or files.

Non-personalized ads are served via Google AdMob. You can opt out of interest-based ads in your device settings.

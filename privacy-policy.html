📜 Privacy Policy for Driver Assistant

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Driver Assistant does not collect any personal information or identifiers. All data you enter (vehicle name, odometer readings, maintenance dates) is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any external servers.

2. Permissions

The app only requests notification permission to remind you about upcoming maintenance. It does not request access to camera, location, contacts, or any sensitive data.

3. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized ads. Your activity is not tracked across apps.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you are solely responsible for its protection. We recommend using your device's screen lock to protect your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. You will be notified of any material changes via the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: support@driver-assistant.com
